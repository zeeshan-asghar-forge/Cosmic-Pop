**Cosmic-Pop** is a vibrant, addictive arcade game where you pop colourful celestial objects floating through space. Tap, click, or swipe to burst planets, stars, and comets – build huge combos and unlock cosmic power‑ups.

> *The universe is full of popping fun. How many can you burst before time runs out?*

---

## 📖 Overview

Cosmic-Pop combines fast‑paced matching or tapping mechanics with a soothing space aesthetic. Each level presents a new constellation of objects to pop. Race against the clock, avoid “dud” objects that shrink your score, and trigger chain reactions for massive points.

Current features (subject to change as development continues):
- Tap‑to‑pop gameplay (mouse or touch)
- Multiple object types: planets, stars, asteroids, alien eggs
- Combo system – pop rapidly to multiply your score
- Special power‑ups: time freeze, screen wipe, magnet
- Increasing difficulty and endless or level‑based modes

---

## ✨ Features

- **Simple, intuitive controls** – One tap/click to pop anything.
- **Satisfying feedback** – Particle bursts, sound effects, and screen rumble.
- **Visual variety** – Hand‑drawn or low‑poly space objects with bright colours.
- **Progressive challenge** – Objects move faster and appear in tricky patterns.
- **Mobile & desktop ready** – Works with touch, mouse, or keyboard (optional hotkeys).
